How Prescriptions Work

Three simple steps to
your door.

Whether you're across the street or across the country, getting your compounded prescription filled is straightforward.

01

Upload your script

Snap a photo or upload a PDF of your prescription through our secure online portal. We accept scripts from any registered Australian prescriber.

02

We compound & verify

Our qualified pharmacists review your script, compound your personalised formulation, and conduct rigorous quality checks before dispensing.

03

Delivered to your door

Your prescription is carefully packaged with cold chain compliance where needed and dispatched via express delivery Australia-wide.
